Learn the #1 Constant Mitzvah! Did you know there are six special mitzvot you can fulfill every single moment, no matter where you are or what you're doing? The very first is to know that Hashem (God) exists! This isn't just believing; it's about recognizing and internalizing that God is the Creator of everything and is actively involved in sustaining and guiding the entire world, right now! Take a moment, look around you, and marvel at the intricate design and vastness of the universe, and reflect on the miracle of your own existence. This constant awareness deepens your relationship with the Creator.
