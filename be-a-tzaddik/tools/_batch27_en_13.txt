Learn about the joy of mitzvot! Take a moment to reflect on a good deed you did recently and let yourself feel genuinely happy about it ✡. This special happiness (simcha shel mitzvah) isn't just a bonus - it actually enhances the mitzvah! The Talmud teaches that serving G-d with joy is a fundamental part of Jewish life. When we're happy about doing mitzvot, we draw down even more divine blessings!
