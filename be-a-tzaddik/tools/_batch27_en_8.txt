Discover #2 of the Constant Mitzvot: Don't believe in any other powers! 🚫 This isn't just about avoiding idol worship; it's about deeply internalizing that Hashem is the *only* true power in the universe. Everything – from the smallest atom to the grandest galaxy – is completely under His control. Nothing can act without His permission! Take a moment to recognize that every event, every challenge, and every blessing you experience is directly from Him, with no intermediary forces or independent powers. This profound belief brings immense trust and peace of mind. Source: Exodus 20:3; Rambam, Sefer HaMitzvot, Negative Commandment 2; Mishneh Torah, Avodat Kochavim 2:1.
