Strengthen your focus on G-d! 🎯 Take a moment to understand this fascinating mitzvah: The Rambam explains that even briefly entertaining thoughts about other spiritual powers besides G-d is forbidden. Here's something amazing: This isn't just about idol worship - it's about recognizing that EVERYTHING comes directly from G-d! When something good happens, don't say 'my luck' or 'my zodiac sign' - instead, remember that G-d is the only true power!
