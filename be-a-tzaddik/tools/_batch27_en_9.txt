Do the mitzvah of experiencing both love and awe of G-d! ❤️ Watch a sunset, climb a mountain, feel the rain on your face. These wonders will blow your mind and make you realize how incredibly small and insignificant you are compared to the Creator. It's like loving a majestic lion – you're captivated by its beauty and power, but you also know you wouldn't want to mess with it. This awe-inspiring experience will fill you with love and a healthy dose of fear, reminding you of God's incredible might.
