Learn some halacha about the Shema - If you're at work when it's time to say Shema, and you can't say it later, here's what to do: Take a complete break from your work for the whole first paragraph. Whether you're self-employed or working for someone else, this special mitzvah takes priority! After that first section, you can multitask and say the rest while working. Even if you're on the clock, G-d comes first!
