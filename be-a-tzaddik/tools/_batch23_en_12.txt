Explore the foundations of faith! ✡ The Rambam teaches something amazing: The very first mitzvah isn't just to believe in G-d, but to KNOW there is a G-d. How do we do this? Look at creation with wonder - from the precise orbits of planets to the complexity of a single cell! Just like you know the house you're in had a builder without seeing them, the intricate design of the universe proves there's a Creator! Take a moment to notice the incredible wisdom in G-d's creation around you.
