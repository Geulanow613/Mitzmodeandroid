Spread some smiles: Say a kind word! 🗣️👂 The mitzvah of greeting people with warmth and kindness (Hachnassat Orchim - welcoming guests, and general acts of chesed) is a daily opportunity to brighten someone's day immeasurably! A genuine smile, a friendly 'Shalom,' or a thoughtful word of encouragement can transform someone's experience. The Sages teach that even a small act of kindness has profound spiritual value. Who in your life could use a word of upliftment today? Make it your mission to spread some positivity!
