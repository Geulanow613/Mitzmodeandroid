Saw a lost object recently? 🔍 Don't just ignore it! Go back and try to return it to its owner (if possible). That's the mitzvah of Hashavat Aveidah (returning lost objects). The Torah teaches that if you see your fellow's animal or belongings wandering off, you may not look away — you must make an effort to return them. Our sages devoted entire discussions to this mitzvah: how hard you must try, what you can keep as a reward for your trouble, and why restoring someone's property is also restoring their dignity.
