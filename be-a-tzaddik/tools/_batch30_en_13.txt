Say/text something nice to someone else or do a nice act for them. An act of kindness with no ulterior motives (except for scoring yet another mitzvah! Cha-Ching!) Our sages teach that we are meant to walk in G-d's ways — just as He clothed Adam and Eve and visited Abraham when he was ill, so must we care for others. Rabbi Akiva called loving your fellow Jew a great principle of the entire Torah. A sincere word of encouragement can lift someone's whole day — and sometimes that one kind act is exactly what they needed to keep going.
