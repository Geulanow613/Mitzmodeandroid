# Halachic disclaimer

Be a Tzaddik is an **educational checklist tool**. It is not a substitute for a rabbi, posek, or personal halachic guidance.

- Wait times for meat and dairy, prayer obligations, and holiday practices vary by community and personal situation.
- Always follow local customs and an Orthodox rabbi you trust before relying on this app for halachic decisions.
- Location-based times are approximate; use your community's published zmanim when precision matters.
- On **Shabbat and Yom Tov**, the app shows a rest screen and asks you not to use electronics, based on your saved location and timezone.
